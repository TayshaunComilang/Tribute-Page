Josephine "Becca" Agatlao Comilang

A Life of Love and Kindness

Josephine "Becca" Agatlao Comilang (March 1957 – March 19, 2024) dedicated her life to teaching, inspiring generations of students at Pacalso Elementary School and other schools during her career. She loved teaching mother tongue (kankanaey) and believed in the power of education to change lives.

Known for her generosity, she often helped many struggling families and students, ensuring that they could graduate including those who were not her own children. She supported them financially, emotionally and even by providing a home when needed. She would give everything she had to help others even if it meant having nothing left for herself.

Beyond teaching, Grandma loved dancing, playing table tennis and singing, filling every room with joy and warmth. Her spirit and love will always remain in the hearts of those she touched.

A Glimpse into My Beloved Grandma’s Life

  • March 1957: Born in the Philippines.
  • 1980s: Began her teaching career as Teacher 1 at various elementary schools.
  • 1990s: Promoted to Teacher 2 and continued to inspire students.
  • 2000s: Became Teacher 3, dedicating over 30 years to shaping young minds.
  • 2002: Adopted a child from a struggling family, demonstrating her generosity and boundless kindness.
  • March 19, 2024:She died suddenly from a stroke in the hospital while caring for her husband there. Leaving behind a legacy of love, teaching and compassion.
